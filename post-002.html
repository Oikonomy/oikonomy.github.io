Top social media to help your busieness grow.

Posted on May 21, 2020

After we knowing about the important of using social media for business. We need to know what is the next step we should do or choose. Choosing the right social media platform that relate to your business is important, in order to get the right customers. There is the most popular social media in the number of online user that potentially increase your business brand.

Facebook

Facebook with 2.41 billion daily active users, make facebook be number one of the most popular social platform. Facebook with a lot of variety of user make it suitable for any various business model. With using facebook page for your business, you can organize your business profile that can which can be easily accessed by everyone. User can like the pages to get seeing updates from your page. In facebook page you can easily acces to promote your content in order to reach more potential customers.

Instagram

Instagram comes with number two on our list of social media sites for business. Instagram is owned by the same person who owns Facebook, and has 500 million daily active users. With main focus in sharing content in post, stories, and IGTV. In instagram, you can create a business account for your business itself in order to to increase the market of your business.

Youtube

YouTube is a most famous video platform with 2 billion monthly active users, and the second most popular social platform. In youtube you can giving your business the chance to share company content with youtube active user. That will really helpfull to places your videos content in convinient way that people likes.

Pinterest

If your business target are in the creative industry or something like that,Pinterest are social media platform that can introduce or educate your customers about your products. With developing the quality of your business content, you can share about information, inspires ideas, and eye-catching content to attract new customers.

LinkedIn

Because LinkedIn is considered to be a social media platform for business, people often include more accurate information about their employers and works than other social media platforms.

Tiktok

is a new comers in the social media platform that comes right after instagram from the total of active users. Tiktok provide services based video and visual that user can create and share. Like writing a blog that related with your products, you can share an information that related in your business in order to increase the number of your potential customers.

Whatsapp

whatsapp come in the third most used social media platform by number of online user. With using whatsapp, will keep your business stay close with your customers. You can easily interacts using chat or call, send reminders, and send promotional offers about your products. Besides using email or SMS to interact with your customers. Whatsapp provide amazing two-sided service that create effective and simple way to keep your business stay connected with the customers.
